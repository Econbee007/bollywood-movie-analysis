Katti Batti is an anti-love story starring Imran Khan as Madhav Kabra (Maddy) &amp; Kangana Ranaut as Payal. Maddy is an architect who is in love with Payal because of her attitude towards life. The story revolves around Maddy &amp; Payal's live-in relationship for 5 years until a sudden turn of events when Payal leaves Maddy mid-way!
